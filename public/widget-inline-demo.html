Inline Embed Demo

This page shows how to mount the KangenChat widget inline. Change theme props or swap between inline/floating modes by editing the snippet below, then reload. 

window.KangenChat.init({
  mode: 'inline',
  containerId: 'inline-slot',
  primaryColor: '#024F9E',
  logoUrl: 'https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/16/Enagic_logo.png',
  welcomeTitle: 'Enagic Support',
  welcomeSubtitle: 'Water experts are online'
});
